Wanjoo West

The better suburb

Features: Stadium

A stadium in a suburb will stimulate local economic growth by attracting visitors, creating jobs, and encouraging the development of nearby businesses like restaurants, hotels, and shops. Most importantly, it will create crucial infrastructure, laying the foundation for ongoing community development and connectivity. This multifaceted benefit transforms the area into a vibrant hub of activity and economic opportunity.

Emergency Services

Wanjoo West takes immense pride in its exceptional emergency services, a cornerstone of the suburb's commitment to safety and community well-being. The emergency response centre is equipped with cutting-edge technology and staffed by highly trained professionals who are always ready to respond swiftly and efficiently to any crisis, whether it's a medical emergency, fire, or other urgent situation. This proactive approach not only provides residents with peace of mind but also fosters a culture of preparedness and resilience through regular safety drills and awareness programs.

Features: Statue of Appreciation

We erected a statue to Honor the dedication and hard work of those who contributed to the model. This statue symbolizes unity and serves as an inspiration for future generations. We hope that this tribute will encourage them to uphold and continue this legacy of excellence and perseverance. The statue stands as a testament to our collective effort, a reminder of what we can achieve when we come together, and an enduring symbol of our shared commitment to progress. This monument will inspire pride and motivation in our community for years to come.
